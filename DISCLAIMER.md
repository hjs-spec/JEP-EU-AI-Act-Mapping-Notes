## Deployment Responsibility

Compliance with the EU AI Act depends on:
1. Correct risk classification of your AI system
2. Proper implementation of conformity assessment (Annex VI or VII)
3. Accurate technical documentation (Art. 11 + Annex IV)
4. Valid CE marking and EU database registration (Art. 49, 71)
5. Ongoing post-market monitoring (Art. 72)

JEP provides **auditable evidence** for items 1, 3, and 4 above. It does not replace legal review by qualified counsel.
